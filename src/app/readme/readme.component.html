Business Card App

Welcome to Bizzy! Have you ever found yourself at a networking event or meeting with potential clients but don't have any business cards on hand? Perhaps searching for a specific business card from a potential employer to no avail? With Bizzy, add and store all your business connections in one place in an easy online collection.

You can add card information manually, or for an even easier option you can take a picture of the physical card and the information will be automatically read, extracted, and placed into your card collection. Navigate to "My Cards" to view your collection of business cards. While there you can both update the individual fields of a card or delete the card altogether.

Enjoy!
